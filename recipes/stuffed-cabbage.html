Stuffed Cabbage Rolls

Description

My grandmother made this for my Dad and and my Mom made this regularly for everyone.

This was never my favorite, so I don't have the recipe. Going from (rough) memory here. I distinctly remember the smell of these cooking, though.

Ingredients

  • cabbage
  • ground beef
  • rice

Steps

  1. Mix rice and beef
  2. make balls from the mix, and wrap them in cabbage leaves. I think you have to tie them up with string.
  3. Put the wrapped balls in the oven, and bake for a while.
  4. cook until the house smells like cabbage
  5. Try to get someone to eat these. Good luck!
