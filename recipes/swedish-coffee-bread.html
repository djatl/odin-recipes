Swedish Coffee Bread

Description

My grandmother used to make this regularly and I've made it a few times with my mother. Even my kids have helped out. I don't want the recipe to disappear, so here's the instructions.

Ingredients

  • milk
  • eggs
  • flour
  • yeast

Steps

  1. Mix everything together
  2. Let it rise
  3. make long skinny tubes
  4. let it rise again
  5. braid the tubes (3 at a time)
  6. bake it
